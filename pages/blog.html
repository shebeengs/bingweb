Starting my Day as a Cat

Posted on December 7, 2020.

There are only 2 important things when you're a cat -- eating and sleeping (and maybe playing).

To start the day, you wake up your human at 3 AM. It doesn't matter if she only slept 20 mins ago. Food time is food time. Waking up your human is simple: just scream as loud as you can on her face.

And that's how you get food.
